Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you visit our website, join our waitlist, or use our product.

Summary: We collect minimal data, never sell it, and use it only to provide the service.

1. Information We Collect

On the website:

  • Pages you visit
  • Time spent on pages
  • Device type and browser
  • Referral source

On the waitlist:

  • Email address
  • Signup date
  • Traffic source

When using the product:

  • Email address
  • Website URL where you're running tests
  • Aggregated performance metrics
  • Billing information (processed by Stripe)

What we DON'T collect:

  • Personal data of your website visitors
  • Individual user behavior
  • Sensitive information

2. How We Use Your Information

Your email address is used to:

  • Send product updates
  • Account notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries

You can unsubscribe anytime.

We never sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for unrelated purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Servers in EU
  • Encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
  • Encrypted at rest
  • Only core team members have access

4. Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Essential: Session management, security
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data, no cross-site tracking

We don't use advertising or social media tracking cookies.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you're in the EU, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Object to processing

To exercise these rights, email privacy@sampelit.com

6. Data Retention

  • Waitlist emails: Until you unsubscribe or we launch
  • Customer data: Duration of subscription + 30 days
  • Analytics: 12 months
  • Billing records: 7 years (legal requirement)

7. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email privacy@sampelit.com
