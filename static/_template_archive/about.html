Copy testing infrastructure for teams that prefer data over opinions.

Most landing page decisions are made in meetings. We built a system that lets the data decide instead.

What we built

A testing system specifically designed for copy optimization. Not a full-stack A/B testing platform. Not a visual editor for layouts. Just copy.

You define variants. We distribute traffic intelligently. Results are evaluated only when statistically meaningful.

The methodology is based on adaptive allocation—variants that perform well receive more traffic automatically. This reduces the cost of testing by minimizing exposure to underperforming copy.

How we work

We use our own product

This page is part of an active experiment. You're seeing one of several variants. We test our own copy before asking you to test yours.

Every decision is auditable

Allocation decisions are logged with cryptographic hashes. You can verify that results were not manipulated after the fact.

We publish our methodology

Statistical confidence thresholds, minimum sample sizes, allocation algorithms. The approach is documented. The implementation is proprietary.

We don't promise improvements

Testing reveals which copy performs better. Sometimes the difference is negligible. That's a valid result. We won't sell you on guaranteed uplift.

What this isn't

Not a full A/B testing suite. We don't test layouts, colors, or complex UI changes. Only text.

Not for low-traffic sites. Reliable results require at least 2,000 monthly visitors.

Not a marketing agency. We provide infrastructure. You bring the copy and the hypotheses.

Not enterprise-priced. €49/month. No sales calls. No custom contracts.
