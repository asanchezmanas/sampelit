Sampelit is an intelligent A/B testing platform that uses Multi-Armed Bandit algorithms to automatically optimize your experiments. Unlike traditional A/B testing, our adaptive algorithms shift traffic to winning variants in real-time, maximizing conversions while gathering statistically significant data.

Multi-Armed Bandit (MAB) balances exploration with exploitation. Unlike A/B tests that show variants equally, MAB dynamically allocates more traffic to winners, reducing opportunity cost while achieving statistical significance.
