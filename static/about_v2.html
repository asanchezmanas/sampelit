The Spark

Why we built this

We were running a SaaS company. Every landing page decision was a debate: emotional vs. rational copy, "Start Trial" vs. "Get Started". We had opinions, but no data.

We wanted something simpler, faster, and designed specifically for copy testing. So we built it.
