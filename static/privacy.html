Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you visit our website, join our waitlist, or use our product.

Summary: We collect minimal data, never sell it, and use it only to provide the service.

1. Information We Collect

On the website:

  • Pages you visit
  • Time spent on pages
  • Device type and browser
  • Referral source

On the waitlist:

  • Email address
  • Signup date
  • Traffic source

What we DON'T collect:

  • Personal data of your website visitors
  • Individual user behavior
  • Sensitive information

2. How We Use Your Information

Email address: Product updates, account notifications, responding to inquiries. You can unsubscribe anytime.

We never: Sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for unrelated purposes.

3. Cookies

We use minimal cookies: essential (session, security) and analytics (anonymous usage). We don't use advertising or social media tracking.

4. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you're in the EU, you can: access, correct, delete, export your data, or object to processing. Email privacy@samplit.com.

5. Data Retention

  • Waitlist emails: Until you unsubscribe or we launch
  • Customer data: Duration of subscription + 30 days
  • Analytics: 12 months

6. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email: privacy@samplit.com
