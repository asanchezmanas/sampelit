Setting Up Your First A/B Test with AI

schedule Last updated 2 days ago
timer 5 min read
v2.4.0 Compatible

Learn how to launch your first automated experiment in minutes using our AI-driven engine. This guide covers installation, goal setting, and parameter configuration to ensure your data is statistically significant from day one.

1. Installation

The first step is to include the tracking snippet in the <head> of your application. This lightweight script (12kb) initializes the AI engine without blocking the main thread.

index.html 
<!-- Sampelit SDK -->
<script src="https://cdn.sampelit.com/sdk/v2/loader.js" data-api-key="YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY" async></script>
<script>
  window.sampelit = window.sampelit || [];
  sampelit.init({
    mode: 'auto',
    trafficAllocation: 0.5 // 50% traffic
  });
</script>
info

Important Note

Ensure you replace YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY with the key found in your project settings dashboard. Do not use your secret key on the client side.

2. Conversion Goals

Once the script is active, you need to define what success looks like. You can track button clicks, page views, or custom events. The visual editor allows you to tag elements without writing code.

3. AI Parameters

Configure how aggressive the AI should be when declaring a winner. Lower confidence thresholds yield faster results but higher risk of false positives.

  • check_circle
    Conservative (99% confidence): Best for critical checkout flows where revenue is at risk.
  • check_circle
    Balanced (95% confidence): Recommended for most UI/UX experiments.
  • check_circle
    Aggressive (90% confidence): Useful for low-traffic pages or rapid iteration cycles.
