Shopify Integration Guide

Optimize your e-commerce store with Samplit's native Shopify integration. Track conversions, A/B test product pages, and boost your revenue automatically.

schedule Last updated Dec 26, 2025
timer 14 min read
Shopify App v3.0

Overview

The Samplit Shopify integration allows you to run A/B tests on your store with automatic tracking of conversions, orders, and customer behavior. We use Shopify's OAuth architecture and Script Tags to ensure a seamless "no-code" installation that works across all themes.

check_circle One-click app installation
check_circle Automatic revenue tracking
check_circle No theme core editing
check_circle Real-time webhook events

Quick Start

  1. Log into your Samplit Dashboard.
  2. Navigate to Integrations â†’ Add Shopify.
  3. Enter your store's .myshopify.com domain.
  4. Click Connect and authorize the app in your Shopify Admin.
  5. The tracker and webhooks will be configured automatically.

OAuth & Security

Samplit requests only the necessary permissions to run and track experiments:

Scope Purpose
read_products Syncing product data for visual editing
read_orders Tracking conversion revenue and order status
write_script_tags Injecting the tracker without modifying liquid files

Tracking Conversions

The following events are tracked out-of-the-box with any theme:

  • order_completed: Full Order Value, items, and tax are captured via webhooks.
  • checkout_started: Tracked when a user initiates the checkout process.
  • add_to_cart: Tracked on product pages and AJAX cart updates.
  • product_viewed: Tracked on all Shopify product templates.

Troubleshooting

warning

Domain Error

Ensure you use your .myshopify.com domain (e.g., mystore.myshopify.com) during setup, even if you have a custom primary domain like www.mystore.com.
