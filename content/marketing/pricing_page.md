## FAQ

### Can I change plans later?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. Upgrades are prorated.

### What happens if I exceed my visitor limit?

We'll notify you at 80% and 100% usage. You can upgrade or wait for the next billing cycle. We don't cut your experiments mid-test.

### Do you offer refunds?

No refunds for partial months. You can cancel anytime and access continues until the end of your billing period.

### Is there a free trial?

Yes. 14-day free trial on Starter and Professional plans. No credit card required to start.

### What counts as a "visitor"?

A unique visitor who sees at least one experiment variant. We count unique users per month, not page views.

### Do you support SPAs and dynamic sites?

Yes. Our snippet works with React, Vue, Angular, and all major frameworks.

### Can I export my data?

Yes. Full CSV export anytime. Your data belongs to you.

### Is this GDPR compliant?

Yes. We don't collect personal data from your visitors. Only aggregated metrics.
