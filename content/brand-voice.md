## Formatting Rules

1. **No exclamation marks** in headlines or primary copy
2. **Sentence case** for headlines (not Title Case)
3. **Short paragraphs** (2-3 sentences max)
4. **White space** is intentional (don't fill every pixel)
5. **Links are subtle** (no "CLICK HERE" buttons)
