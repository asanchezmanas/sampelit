# Copywriting Science

Research-backed content about the psychology of words, neuromarketing, and decision-making.

## Purpose
- Differentiate from competitors (most talk tactics, we talk science)
- Attract high-intent, educated audience
- Position as thought leaders
- Build credibility through academic references

## Target Audience
- Marketing professionals interested in psychology
- Founders who value data and research
- Academics and researchers (secondary)
- Copywriters looking to level up

## Articles

### 01. The Neuroscience of Persuasive Copy: What Brain Science Tells Us
**Target Keyword:** "neuroscience of persuasion"
**Secondary:** "neuromarketing copywriting", "brain and advertising"

**Key Research to Cover:**
- Kahneman's System 1 vs System 2 thinking
- Emotional vs rational processing in purchase decisions
- The role of the amygdala in decision-making
- fMRI studies on ad effectiveness

**Papers:**
- Kahneman, D. (2011). Thinking, Fast and Slow
- Damasio, A. (1994). Descartes' Error
- Knutson et al. (2007). Neural predictors of purchases
