# Blog Content Strategy

This folder contains all blog content for Samplit, organized by content pillar.

## Content Pillars

### 1. `ab-testing-fundamentals/`
Educational content about A/B testing. SEO-focused, targeting keywords like "A/B testing guide", "how to run A/B tests", "A/B testing mistakes".

**Goal:** Establish authority, attract organic traffic from people researching testing.

### 2. `case-studies/`
Analysis of our own experiments. Transparent, data-driven.

**Goal:** Build trust, demonstrate expertise, show real results (including failures).

### 3. `copywriting-science/`
Research-backed content about the psychology of words, neuromarketing, decision-making.

**Goal:** Differentiate from competitors, position as thought leaders, attract high-intent traffic.

---

## Publishing Schedule (Suggested)

- Week 1: 1x Fundamentals article
- Week 2: 1x Case Study
- Week 3: 1x Science article
- Week 4: 1x Fundamentals article

Repeat. Aim for 4 articles/month minimum.

---

## SEO Guidelines

1. Each article should target 1 primary keyword
2. Include related long-tail keywords naturally
3. Use clear H2/H3 structure
4. Include internal links to other articles and product pages
5. Add external links to reputable sources (especially in Science pillar)
6. Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences)
7. Include practical takeaways / actionable advice
