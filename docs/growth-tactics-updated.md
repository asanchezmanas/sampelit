## 2. Programa de Referrals

### Estructura

```
Cliente refiere → Nuevo cliente paga primer mes → Ambos reciben 1 mes gratis
```

**Valor para Sampelit:**
- CAC de referral: €149-399 (1 mes gratis)
- vs CAC de ads: €200-300
- Clientes referidos tienen mejor retention

### Implementación

**Email automático post-signup (día 30):**
```
Subject: ¿Conoces a alguien que podría usar Sampelit?

Hey [Name],

Ya llevas un mes usando Sampelit. Si conoces a alguien 
que podría beneficiarse, comparte tu link:

[referral_link]

Si se suscriben, ambos recibís un mes gratis.

Saludos,
[Founder name]
```

**Tracking:** UTM `?ref=user_[user_id]`

### In-app Referral

- Widget en dashboard: "Refer & earn free months"
- Después de experimento exitoso: "Share your results?"
- Settings page: referral link siempre visible

## 3. LinkedIn Organic

### Estrategia

- **Frecuencia**: 3-4 posts/semana
- **Formato**: Text-only performa mejor que links
- **Tono**: Educational, contrarian, no promotional
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 máximo (#ABtesting #CRO #StartupGrowth)

### Templates de Posts

**Formato 1: Contrarian take**
```
Most people think A/B testing is about finding winners.

It's not.

It's about reducing uncertainty.

Most tests are inconclusive. That's okay.
The value is in the process, not just the wins.

What's the last "failed" test that taught you something?
```

**Formato 2: Micro case study**
```
We ran a headline test on our pricing page.

Original: "Simple A/B Testing"
Variant: "A/B Testing Without the Enterprise Price Tag"

Results after 2,847 visitors:
- Original: 4.2% signup rate
- Variant: 5.8% signup rate

+38% improvement.

The lesson: Specificity beats vague benefits.

What's working in your copy tests?
```

**Formato 3: Data/insight**
```
Analyzed 50 headline tests from our platform.

The patterns:

→ Specific numbers beat vague claims (78% of cases)
→ "How to" beats "Why you should" (65% of cases)
→ Shorter headlines win on landing pages (71% of cases)
→ Longer headlines win in email subjects (58% of cases)

Context matters more than rules.
```

## 6. Product Hunt Launch

### Timing

- NO lanzar antes de tener 10+ clientes pagando
- Preferible: después de 3-6 meses, con social proof

### Preparación (2 semanas antes)

- [ ] 50+ upvotes comprometidos (amigos, early users)
- [ ] Video demo de 2 minutos
- [ ] 5+ "Maker comments" preparados
- [ ] Offer especial para PH (20% off primer año)
- [ ] Responder comments en las primeras 4 horas

### Expectativas realistas

- Top 5 del día: 500-1000 visitantes
- Conversión a trial: 5-10%
- Trial a paid: 20-30%
- **Resultado esperado:** 5-15 clientes pagando

## Prioridades por Fase

### Fase 1: 0-20 clientes (Validación)
1. LinkedIn organic (gratis, construye audiencia)

1. Escalar content (4 posts/mes)

3. Lanzar programa referrals

### Fase 3: 50-100 clientes (Escala)
1. Escalar ads (€1,500+/mes)
2. Product Hunt launch
3. Partnerships activos
4. Considerar primer hire (content/support)
