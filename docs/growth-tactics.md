## 4. LinkedIn Organic

### Estrategia

- **Frecuencia**: 3 posts/semana
- **Formato**: Text-only performa mejor que links
- **Tono**: Educational, not promotional
- **Voz**: Anti-hype, datos, contrarian

### Templates

**Formato 1: Contrarian take**
```
Most people think A/B testing is about finding winners.

It's not.

It's about reducing uncertainty.

Most tests are inconclusive. That's okay.
The value is in the process, not just the wins.
```

**Formato 2: Micro case study**
```
We ran a headline test on our landing page.

Variant A: "A/B Testing for Copy"
Variant B: "Test Your Headlines, Not Your Patience"

Results after 10,000 visitors:
- A: 3.2% conversion
- B: 3.1% conversion

Difference: Not statistically significant.

What we learned: Sometimes the "boring" option is just as good.
```

**Formato 3: Data point**
```
We analyzed 500 A/B tests.

The average lift of a "winner"? 12%.

But here's what nobody talks about:

68% of tests showed no significant difference.

The 32% that did? Those paid for all the inconclusive ones.

Testing is a volume game.
```
